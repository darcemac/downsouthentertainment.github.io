About

Lee Cummings - Founder / Director

Lee Cummings has been surfing and playing music around the South West for over 20 years. During this time he has come to be recognised as the vibrant, fun-loving and cheeky character behind some of the region’s most successful bands, memorable parties and notable events.

A successful world-travelled percussionist (see L.C.DRUMS ), Lee spent a growing amount of time in the South West playing the role of go-to-guy for music and booking recommendations. It was the increasing demand for his entertainment booking advice and overwhelming positive feedback that led to Lee founding Down South Entertainment in 2011.

DSE is steadily gaining momentum throughout the South West as more venues, artists, wedding couples and event organisers make use of Lee’s expertise, which is enhanced by his unmistakable “Down South” vibe.

"Music is life, life is music."
